  1. How many times have you used the printers at the Sci Li?
  2. How easy was the process of printing your papers?

  4. Was the process easier or more difficult than expected?
The Problem: The interface here is trying to solve the problem of making the printing process easier and quicker for students. Here one can add to the printing queue from their local computer or from the ‘Computers to Access area and once they go ahead and swipe their card it will appear on the Printing Display and they may proceed with the printing process. This allows for the payment, person, and printout to all be done through a single swipe of the card.
